Garden facilities and green spaces

If arranged qualitatively, the value of a garden will increase over time.

Arrangement stages:

  1. Land infrastructure ( stripping, milling, compaction )
  2. Making slopes and valonaments ( if applicable )
  3. Installation of irrigation and drainage systems
  4. Planting and lawning
  5. Installation of pergolas and wooden deck for exterior
  6. Aesthetic arrangement with bark or stone
