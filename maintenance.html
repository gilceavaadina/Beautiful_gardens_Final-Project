Maintenance of green spaces

What does garden maintenance include?

Pond
  • Periodic cleaning of filters
  • Emptying and cleaning the lake with substances that do not affect flora and fauna
  • Application of substances necessary for maintaining PH and oxygenating water
  • Periodic cleaning of leaves and deposits
  • Installation of required or additional UV filters
  • Periodic cleaning of leaves and deposits
Irrigation system
  • Emptying the system at the end of autumn and filling it in spring
  • springjustment and reprogramming
  • Cleaning of filters and sprinkler nozzles
  • Adjustment of aspersion angles or their relocation ( vegetation function )
  • Finding and replacing worn or damaged components
Lawn
  • Tunde
  • scarification
  • Aeration
  • Foliar and organic fertilization, basic or specific
  • Treatment against diseases and pests over-insant
Plants
  • Toilets, training cuts, regeneration and more
  • Periodic cleaning of dry parts, leaves, branches, etc.
  • Basic and specific foliar and organic fertilizers
  • Periodic cleaning of the leaves
