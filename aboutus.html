Our solution

Garden design can seem like a complicated process. That's why we did our best to simplify it!

  • Contact us and give us a brief description of your garden project
  • We set up a meeting and offer you consulting in the first phase
  • Our architects design the landscape project
  • We buy everything that is included in the project and we offer you the technical solutions, together with the stages of realization
  • We make the complete arrangement of the green space(infrastructure, watering system, planting, lawning )
  • We also take care of the maintenance of your future garden
